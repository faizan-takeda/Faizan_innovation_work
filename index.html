OdyssAI

AI driven drug discovery acceleration engine

Inspired by Odyssey, a long and complex journey, much like drug discovery.

mAP 0.81 Kaggle Benchmark

Context

Drug discovery requires accurate and scalable prediction of molecular interactions and biological outcomes.

Innovation

Hybrid modeling integrating biophysical simulations, chemical descriptors, and deep learning to predict binding affinity across BRD4, HSA, and SEH. It also incorporates Lipinski Rule of Five, CNS molecular descriptors, and structural bioactivity signals.

Impact

Achieved mAP 0.81 on Kaggle benchmark and optimized architecture to train efficiently on millions of molecular data points.
